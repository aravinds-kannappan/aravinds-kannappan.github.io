An open-source AI safety evaluation framework that benchmarks frontier LLMs at the intersection of adversarial robustness and model interpretability.

**Key work:**
- Designed experiments measuring alignment stability across semantically equivalent question variants (6 variants per indicator)
- Built statistical analysis pipeline: Likert histograms, cross-model EDA, and semantic analysis of chain-of-thought justifications across 50+ safety-relevant indicators
- Developed mathematical models characterizing ground-truth drift in LLM judgment across model generations
- Framework designed for longitudinal alignment drift tracking and integration with human expert survey data

[GitHub](https://github.com/aravinds-kannappan)

**Stack:** Python, PyTorch, HuggingFace, statistical analysis, RLHF evaluation
