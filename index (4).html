Technical Blog

  • Chess, Minimax & Alpha-Beta Pruning: A Graph-Theoretic ML Lens
    Algorithms · Game Theory · Search Trees
    Chess as a DAG with branching factor ~35. We derive the minimax recurrence, prove the α-β pruning bound O(b^(d/2)), and draw the line from expectiminimax to MCTS — the backbone of AlphaZero's policy rollouts.
  • The Spectral View of Gradient Descent: Why Conditioning Beats Learning Rate Tuning
    Optimization · Linear Algebra · Deep Learning Theory
    Eigendecompose the Hessian ∇²L to show why ill-conditioned landscapes stall training. We derive the condition number κ = λ_max/λ_min, its effect on the optimal step size, and why Adam's per-parameter scaling is implicit preconditioning.
  • Markov Chains on Traffic Networks: Stochastic Matrices, Stationarity & PageRank
    Probability · Graph Theory · Applied Statistics
    Model urban intersections as a finite Markov chain. Prove existence of stationary distribution π via Perron-Frobenius, derive its equivalence to Google's PageRank, and bound mixing time — directly relevant to real-time routing algorithms.
  • Variational Inference as Optimization: KL Divergence, the ELBO, and Why VAEs Work
    Bayesian Methods · Information Theory · Generative Models
    Derive the ELBO from Jensen's inequality: log p(x) ≥ E_q[log p(x,z)] − KL(q‖p). Cover the reparameterization trick as a variance reduction method, posterior collapse, and the β-VAE KL annealing fix.
  • Suffix Arrays, BWT & Sequence Alignment: When Algorithms Meet Genomics
    String Algorithms · Bioinformatics · Computational Biology
    The Burrows-Wheeler Transform as lossless compression with O(n) suffix array construction. Show how BWA-MEM exploits BWT to align reads to a 3B base-pair genome in sublinear expected time, and why this underpins modern variant calling.
